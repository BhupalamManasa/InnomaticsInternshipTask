About Us

Welcome, we appreciate you wanting to learn more about how we’ve become the family owned and operated business we are today. In 1989 Brewed Awakenings was created at a time when coffee houses and drive-thru’s were in the infancy stages and only a handful existed. Our premise was to buy and serve the best quality coffee and pastries available. Over the years we have continued serving the best quality coffee from around the world. Our obsession for coffee continues today. We have developed unique coffee roasts and blends on our Probat Roaster. We have also developed our own pastry recipes using the highest quality ingredients and baked fresh daily by our professionally trained bakers.
